Mr. Yuan LongPing

This man has supported 1.4 billion people in China

Here's a time line of Yuan LongPing's life:

  • 1930 - Yuan LongPing was born in Peking Union Medical College Hospital
  • 1971—1984 - Researcher of Rice Institute of Hunan Academy of Agricultural Sciences, member of provincial hybrid rice research collaboration group
  • 1979 - He served as a member of the Science and Technology Committee of the Ministry of Agriculture, vice chairman of the Chinese Crop Society, director of the Chinese Genetic Society, director of the Hunan Biological Society, and was awarded the title of Chinese Advanced Scientist and Chinese Model Worker by the State Council, vice chairman of the Hunan Genetic and Breeding Society, and director of the Hunan Agricultural Society
  • 1982 - He was employed as the technical consultant of the Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry and Fisheries, and the deputy leader of the Chinese hybrid rice expert advisory group
  • 1984 - Hunan Hybrid Rice Research Center was established, and Yuan LongPing served as the director of the center
  • 1985 - He was employed as honorary president of Hunan AnJiang Agricultural University and part-time professor of Southwest Agricultural University
  • 1988—1995 - Vice Chairman of Hunan CPPCC, Director of Hunan Hybrid Rice Research Center, Honorary President of Hunan Academy of Agricultural Sciences
  • 1991 - Honorary President of Hunan Academy of Agricultural Sciences. In the same year, he was employed as the international chief adviser of FAO
  • 1995—2016 - Vice Chairman of the CPPCC Hunan Provincial Committee, Director of the National Hybrid Rice Engineering Technology Research Center (elected as an academician of the CAE Member in 1995)
  • 2003 - The People's Government of Hainan Province held a ceremony in Sanya to hire Academician Yuan LongPing as the senior scientific and technological adviser of Hainan Provincial Government
  • 2005 - Engaged by Guizhou Provincial People's Government as a consultant of Guizhou Academy of Agricultural Sciences
  • 2008 - Honorary Dean of Hunan Biomechanics Vocational and Technical College
  • 2012 - Yuan LongPing was formally employed as "Chen Guangbiao Senior Advisor of Green Food Industry"
  • 2016 - Yuan LongPing Resigns as Vice Chairman of Hunan Provincial CPPCC
  • 2018 - Elected as "Member" of China Invention Association
  • 2019 - Yuan LongPing, President of the Council of the International High end Agricultural Talent Training Center
  • 2019 - The Third Generation Hybrid Rice Seed Industry Co., Ltd., funded by LongPing Hi Tech, was inaugurated in Changsha, with Yuan LongPing as the chairman. On the same day, Yuan LongPing was invited to serve as the consultant of BeiBei District People's Government of Chongqing
  • 2021 - dies at the age of 91
